About Our Weather Station

Our weather monitoring equipment provides real-time environmental data from the Lost River Field Station. Located at the base of Borah Peak, our station captures the unique high-desert mountain climate of central Idaho. This data helps researchers understand local climate patterns and assists field camp planning.
