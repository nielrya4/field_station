About Borah Peak

Borah Peak stands as Idaho's tallest mountain and is part of the Lost River Range. This live camera provides a continuous view of the peak's changing conditions throughout the day and seasons. The peak is a popular destination for climbers and offers spectacular views of the surrounding wilderness.
