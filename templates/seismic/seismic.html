About the Data

This seismic data is collected from our RaspberryShake station (SAABC) located at the Lost River Field Station. The data shows the last 12 hours of seismic activity, updated automatically every 5 minutes. Raw counts represent the unprocessed signal from the seismometer, while velocity and acceleration show the ground motion after instrument response correction.
